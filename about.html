Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I hire a pet sitter?


By hiring a pet sitter, you can provide your pet with the love, care, and attention they deserve while enjoying the convenience and peace of mind that come with professional pet care services.



Are your pet sitters trained and insured?


Absolutely! The well-being and safety of your pets are our top priorities. All our pet sitters are experienced, trained, and thoroughly vetted. They have a genuine love for animals and are committed to providing the best care possible. We are also fully insured to give you peace of mind.



What types of pets do you provide sitting services for?


We offer pet sitting services for a wide range of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, small mammals (such as rabbits and guinea pigs), and reptiles. We understand that each pet has unique needs, and our experienced sitters are trained to care for various types of animals.



How do I schedule pet sitting services for my pet?


Scheduling pet sitting services is easy! You can either give us a call at (704) 555-1234 or fill out our online booking form on the website. Provide us with your preferred dates and times, and we'll work with you to accommodate your schedule.
